Founding

Someone call the W*nderp*ts!

One day, three friends came across a cat stuck on a tree, "Poor kitty" they thought, So they tried to help the kitty down the tree, First they thought of climbing the tree, but no one can climb the tree. So they bought out cat food to lure the cat into going out on it's own. But it was distracted by birds and the pawril that it was in. Three smore passers-by came and tried various ideas to help the cat down until finally, the cat was brought down with a ladder from the firemen.

A Pawsome Idea is Born!

The group, learning that they've come with a diverse set of skills fit for founding a company over a spontaneous invite of coffee and inspired by their shared experience of pspspspsps-ing their way to help a cat down from the tree, thought of something...

For the love of paws.

Founded in 2020 by 6 friends who all share the same love for their fur-babies. Pawtection is a company built on friendship over the love of paws.
